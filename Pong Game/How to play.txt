How to Play

1)Run the game:

python pong_game.py

2)Choose your gadget pair when prompted.

3)Player 1 controls:

W: Move paddle up
S: Move paddle down
D: Use gadget 1
A: Use gadget 2

4)Player 2 controls:

UP ARROW: Move paddle up
DOWN ARROW: Move paddle down
RIGHT ARROW: Use gadget 1
LEFT ARROW: Use gadget 2

5)The game continues until one player scores 3 points.
